Approach

If it moves, it must move for a reason.

I treat litigation like a structured system: facts, law, procedure, evidence. Every application must earn its place; every hearing must advance the case.

01 • Structure

Chronology first, always

Before drafting: dates, documents, admissions, and the “one-page case theory”.

02 • Procedure

Correct relief, correct forum

Maintainability, limitation, jurisdiction, and interim strategy are checked early.

03 • Drafting

Clean pleadings, strong annexures

Short paragraphs. Proof-backed assertions. No unnecessary fluff.

04 • Execution

Relentless follow-through

Orders are only useful if they are implemented: compliance, execution, next steps.
